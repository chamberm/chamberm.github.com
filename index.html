Welcome! I am a Ph.D. student at the Biomedical Imaging department of Sherbrooke University under the supervision of professors Maxime Descoteaux Ph.D, Kevin Whittingstall Ph.D. and David Fortin M.D. My interests in scientific visualization revolves around real time medical imaging data rendering. I currently work on the development of new visualization methods to display data obtained through fusion of diffusion and functional magnetic resonance imaging (dMRI/fMRI)
