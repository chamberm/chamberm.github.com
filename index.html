Maxime Chamberland

Postdoctoral Research Fellow

Donders Centre for Cognitive Neuroimaging, Radboud University (The Netherlands)

Welcome!

My research interests are driven by a passion to improve our understanding of the human brain by developing methods for diffusion MRI applications.

  • Data Visualization
